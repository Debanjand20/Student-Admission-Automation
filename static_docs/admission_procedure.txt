University Admission Procedure:

1. Candidates must fill out the online application form available on the university website.
2. Submit all required documents: marksheets, ID proof, passport-size photo, and other relevant certificates.
3. Applications are verified by the Admission Office and Document Checking Agent.
4. Shortlisting is based on eligibility criteria and available seats.
5. Selected students may apply for educational loans if needed.
6. Upon approval, students receive an official admission letter and fee slip.
7. Fee payment must be completed by the given deadline to confirm admission.
